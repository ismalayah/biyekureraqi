Chloe Fineman Biography

Chloe Fineman is an American actress, writer, and comedian known for her work on Saturday Night Live in 1975, Search Party in 2016, and High Fidelity in 2020.

She also appeared at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in “Characters Welcome.” She was honored at the Just for Laughs festival in Montreal in 2018 as a “Young Face,” and she was nominated for Best Comedian at the Shorty Awards 2019.
