Career

The choreographer started her career as a child artist with her appearance in the Bollywood movie “Nazarana” in 1952. After that, she started her career as a background dancer and received huge appreciation for her talent and also became one of the respected faces in the Bollywood industry throughout her career.

She had recognized as one of the best choreographers the industry would ever have, and in the back 90’s it was said that dance will not be much perfect without the guidance of Saroj Ji.
