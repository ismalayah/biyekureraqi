Career

Rupert Friend started his career in 2004 by playing the role of “Downs” in a British-Australian drama film titled “The Libertine”.

He played the role of “Mr. Wickham” in a romantic drama film titled “Pride & Prejudice” and appeared as “Ludovic Meyer” in the comedy-drama film “Mrs. Palfrey at the Claremont” in 2005.

In 2007, he was featured as “Renzo Daverio / Spoletta” in the romantic drama film “The Moon and the Stars”, as “Sandy Mardell” in the action thriller film “Outlaw”, as “Demetrius” in the historical adventure film “The Last Legion”, as “Alessandro Felice” in the romantic comedy film “Virgin Territory”.

He portrayed the character “Coco Leger” in the 2008 American drama film “Jolene” and “Lt. Kurt Kotler” in the 2008 historical drama film “The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas”.

He landed the most famous role of “Prince Albert” in the 2009 British period drama film “The Young Victoria”.
