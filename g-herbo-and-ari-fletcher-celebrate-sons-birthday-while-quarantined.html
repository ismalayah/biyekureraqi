G HERBO AND ARI FLETCHER CELEBRATE SONS BIRTHDAY WHILE QUARANTINED

August 23, 2024
blog

G Herbo and Ari Fletcher celebrated their son Yosohn’s second birthday today. For his special day, Ari threw a spider-man themed quarantine party for her little man. The Instagram model made sure to live-stream the event for Yosohn’s more than 200,000 fans.

Gherbo, who couldn’t be in attendance due to coronavirus-related social distancing restrictions, made sure to join the live stream.

Prior to going on live, the rapper wrote on his Instagram, “Been heavy on the Spider man lately. My son is official terrible twooo. He got the quarantine play house going on at the crib cuz he living like it. Happy Birthday. Happy Birthday. We going live later LOL and the fun still ain’t over cuz when outside back open, we viral again!!!”

Yosohn, who was dressed in Spider-Man costume for the celebration, not only enjoyed playing in his play house, but he also had a lot of fun blowing out the candles on his adorable cake.

Mom Ari Fletcher  was the master-mind behind the sweet quarantine celebration. “I can’t even begin to describe what I feel when it comes to how much I love you and how far I’m willing to go and how dangerous,” Fletcher wrote on her Instagram.
