What is Barry Manilow’s Net Worth and Salary?

Barry Manilow is an American singer-songwriter, composer, and producer with a net worth of approximately $100 million. He made his fortune through his successful musical career that spanned over five decades, numerous world tours, and record sales that exceeded 85 million.

Early Life and Education

Barry Manilow was born on June 17, 1943, in Brooklyn, New York. He grew up in a Jewish family, and his parents left him to live with his grandparents when he was just 13 months old. Manilow started playing the piano when he was just seven years old and went on to study music at the Juilliard School of Music.

Career

Manilow began his career playing piano and singing in a CBS recording studio. He then went on to write songs for other artists and produce live events. In 1974, he released his first hit single, “Mandy,” which became his first number one hit in the US.

Since then, he has released more than 30 studio albums, including classic hits such as “Weekend in New England,” “Looks Like We Made It,” and “Copacabana.” He has sold over 85 million records and recorded 11 number one albums on the Billboard 200. He has also been inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame.

Furthermore, he has won numerous accolades throughout his career, including multiple Grammy Awards, Emmy Awards, and American Music Awards. He has also been nominated for a Tony Award for his work in theater.
