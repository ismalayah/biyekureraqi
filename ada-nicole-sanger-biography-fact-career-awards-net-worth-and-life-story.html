Ada-Nicole Sanger (born January 24, 1998) is an American actress from South Florida. She is known for her role as Donna Lamonsoff in Sony Pictures’ 2010 comedy Grown Ups, directed by Dennis Dugan. She was a contestant on the fifth episode of the Nickelodeon game show BrainSurge in October 2009.
